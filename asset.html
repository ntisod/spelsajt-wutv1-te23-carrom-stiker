Game Assets

A breakdown of components used in our digital Carrom game

Core Assets Used

  • Carrom Board: 3D model from Unity Asset Store, styled to reflect an authentic traditional carrom board.
  • Striker: A realistic striker object with Rigidbody and Collider components for smooth flick mechanics.
  • Coins:
    • 8 Black Coins
    • 8 White Coins
    • 1 Queen (Red)
    Each with physics-based interactions for realistic gameplay.

Assets from Unity Asset Store

  • Carrom Board & Pieces Pack – Used for 3D models of the board, coins, and striker.
  • Tabletop Game Materials – Provided realistic surface textures and wood grain appearance.
  • Basic UI Kit – Used to create score display and turn indicators.
  • Simple Sound Effects Pack – For striker hits and potting sound feedback.

Unity Components & Physics

  • Rigidbody: Applied to the striker and coins to enable realistic motion and collisions.
  • Colliders: Used on all interactive objects including pockets, coins, and striker to manage physics-based interactions.
  • Physics Materials: Customized to control bounce, slide, and friction for lifelike carrom dynamics.
  • Scripts: Custom C# scripts manage player input, collision handling, score tracking, and turn logic.

Additional Features

  • Pocket zones with trigger colliders to detect when a coin is potted.
  • Sound effects for striker.
  • User Interface components for score.
