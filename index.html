What is Carrom?

Carrom is a tabletop game that originated in South Asia and is played with small discs called carrom men. Players flick a striker to pocket their pieces into corner pockets. It's a game of skill, strategy, and precision, enjoyed by people of all ages. This site will guide you through the rules, variations, and gameplay techniques to become a Carrom master!
