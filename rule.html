Carrom Game Rules

Objective

The goal is to pot all your assigned coins (either black or white) before your opponent. The player who pots the Queen and covers it correctly gets bonus points.

Basic Rules

  • The game is played between 2 players.
  • Each player is assigned either Black or White coins (8 each).
  • Players take turns to strike using the striker.
  • The Queen (red coin) must be potted and then \"covered\" by potting one of your own coins in the same or next turn.
  • If the Queen is not covered, it is returned to the center of the board.
  • Only the striker is controlled by the player – aim and shoot to pocket coins.

Fouls

  • Pocketing the striker (scratch).
  • Potting the opponent’s coin intentionally.
  • Failing to cover the Queen after potting it.
Winning the Game
  • The first player to pot all of their coins wins the round.
  • If the Queen is potted and successfully covered, that player earns a bonus.
  • In this version, scores may not be tracked yet – the focus is on basic turn-based play.
Note: These rules are simplified for digital play and may vary slightly from official tournament rules.
