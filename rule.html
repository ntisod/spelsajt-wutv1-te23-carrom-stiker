Carrom Game Rules

Objective

The goal is to pot all your assigned coins (either black or white) before your opponent. The player who pots the Queen and covers it correctly gets bonus points.

Basic Rules

  • The game is free play with only one player at the moment.
  • Player is assigned either Black or White coins (8 each).
  • Player strikes using the striker.
  • Pot the red coin (Queen) and pocket any coin immediately after to get 6 points in total.
  • Only the striker is controlled by the player â€” aim and shoot to pocket coins.

Fouls

  • Pocketing the striker.
Winning the Game
  • The first player to reach 9+ points wins.
  • If the Queen is potted and successfully covered, that player earns a bonus of +5 points.
Note: These rules are simplified for digital play and may vary slightly from official tournament rules.
